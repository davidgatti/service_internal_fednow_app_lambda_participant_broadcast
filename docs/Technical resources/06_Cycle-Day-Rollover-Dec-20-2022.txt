FedNow Service funds-transfer
business day

The funds-transfer business day for the FedNow Service as detailed in the FedNow Service Schedule published by the Reserve Banks on the
FRBservices.org® website. Also referred to as “FedNow Service cycle day” or “cycle day.”

Accounting End-of-Day (EOD)
Cycle (~8:00 pm ET)

Timing for when Federal Reserve Accounting has received all debit and credit transactions posting to an institution’s account from all business
lines within the Federal Reserve, to determine the institution’s closing balance for the given cycle date:
• Federal Reserve Accounting will calculate the closing balance (which is also the opening balance for the next cycle date).
• Federal Reserve Accounting will generate the daily Statement of Account & Accounting data files (FIRD and SASF).

Provisional Balance

A provisional balance is provided when FedNow Service/Fedwire Funds have rolled into the new cycle day, but before Federal Reserve
Accounting has completed its end of day cycle process:
• Once Federal Reserve Accounting has calculated the final balance, it will replace the provisional balance.
• FedNow activity, included in the balance, is considered final.
